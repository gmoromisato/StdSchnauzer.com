Canine CPR

On: To Be Determined

This event is still in the Planning Stage with the Instructor. Details will be posted as they become available.

We are planning the class to take place in Dublin/Pleasanton/San Ramon area and on a Sunday. We are looking at a date that will be either late September or early November. Space for this event will be limited and opened to the general dog owning public with SSCNC Members/Applicants having registration priority. There will be a cost to all who attend this training and a limit to the number of people who can enroll. This is one of usually only two no dogs included events SSCNC has per year.

The training will be on the basics of dog first aid and CPR. A great course for anyone who travels, hikes, works, trials or lives with a dog. Learn what to do to stablize a bite wound or treat heat stroke before you can get the dog to a Veterinarian.

Stay tuned as we finalize date and cost for this important learning opportunity.
