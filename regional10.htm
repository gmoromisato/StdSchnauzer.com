FAIRGROUND OVERNIGHT PARKING
On October 13, 2009 the San Francisco Bay Area was deluged by the largest wind and rain storm in 50 years. RV's began rolling onto the Alameda County Fairgrounds on October 15. The grounds were soaked and the Fairgrounds asked us not to use most of the grass areas we usually used for parking. We lost 98 parking spaces. Some of you ended up parking a very long way away from where you wanted to be. We sincerely apologize to you for this inconvenience but it was Mother Nature in charge, not us. With a little luck we'll have back all our normal parking spaces.
