K9 Nose Work

If you want to try scent working with your dog but without the physical rigors of Tracking, try a new sport called “K9 Nose Work” that is just getting started in the United States. Dogs have an amazing sense of smell and a natural desire to hunt. K9 Nose Work takes these natural talents and combines them with rewards and exercise to develop this new sport. It’s a great way for your dog to have fun, build confidence, and burn lots of mental and physical energy. For more information view the National Organization’s web site.
